# Sasha Stoikov

## Affiliation

Cornell University (Operations Research and Information Engineering). Also affiliated with industry research in high-frequency trading and market making.

## Key contributions

- **Avellaneda-Stoikov market making model** (with Marco Avellaneda, 2008) — the canonical stochastic optimal control model of market making. A market maker posts bid and ask quotes dynamically to maximise expected utility, accounting for inventory risk. Widely used by practitioners.
- **Order Flow Imbalance** — co-invented with Cont and Kukanov; empirical validation that OFI drives short-horizon price changes ([[papers/price-impact-order-book-events]]).
- **Mid-price prediction** — work on using LOB features to predict short-horizon price direction.
